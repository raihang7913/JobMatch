# Design Approach Preferences

User explicitly requested: "pakai kreativitas mu untuk me redesign semua tampilannya agar profesional dan keren dan anggaplah kamu adalah seorang frontend designer profesional" (use your creativity to redesign the entire display to be professional and cool, assuming you are a professional frontend designer).

## Validated Design Choices
- **Glassmorphism aesthetic**: Backdrop blur, frosted glass effects, layered transparency
- **Modern typography**: Geist font over Inter, proper hierarchy with bold headings
- **Professional icons**: Phosphor icons with duotone weight - no emojis
- **Circular progress indicators**: For match scores (better than simple badges)
- **Hero sections**: Clear value propositions at the top of each page
- **Skeleton loading states**: Better than spinners or plain text
- **Empty states**: Clear messaging with actionable CTAs

**Why:** User said frontend was "terlalu plain" (too plain) and wanted professional, creative redesign. They approved the glassmorphism approach and modern enhancements.

**How to apply:** For UI work on this or similar projects, lean toward:
- Modern design systems and established patterns (taste-skill guidelines)
- Professional aesthetics that build trust
- Thoughtful micro-interactions and animations
- Real visual hierarchy over flat layouts
- Don't be conservative - user values creative, bold design choices
